Codex depicting Quetzalcoatl

One of Olrox’s vampiric powers is the ability to transform into a giant feathered serpent-like dragon. This form is heavily resemblant of Quetzalcoatl, a diety found in many Mesoamerican religions, such as that of Olrox’s people.

During his time as a human, Spanish conquistadors raided Aztec land, killing many and turning some, like Olrox, into vampires. This tragic history and the loss of his lover, who also fought for freedom, stayed with Olrox even after death, shaping how he lived his ‘life’ as a vampire.

Emerald earrings

In 1792, Olrox, a vampire of over 250 years, was summoned to France by a legion of powerful vampires to celebrate and witness the rise of their queen, Erzsebet Báthory. The ruthless monarch, sought to create a world were vampires ruled over the human population and demanded the subjugation of everyone she believed to be beneath her.

The sentiment that vampires were entirely superior to humans and deserved power over them reminded Olrox of the beliefs that were used to excuse the oppression of his own people centuries ago. This awareness was what led Olrox to secretly disobeying and eventually revolting against Erzsebet and her tyrannical rule.
