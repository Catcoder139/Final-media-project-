Claudia’s ‘Baby Lu’ costume

When Claudia and Louis (her vampiric companion) arrive in Paris in 1945, they are soon taken in by Armand’s coven, who reside at Théâtre Des Vampires. Here, the coven use their vampiric powers, under the guise of being mere stage magic, to perform for mortals and covertly feed on them. Enamoured by their productions, Claudia wished to join the coven on the stage and was eventually cast as ‘Baby Lu’ in ‘My baby loves window’. To her dismay, Claudia was forced to play the role of a little girl; every performance a reminder of the dissonance between her adult mind and child body.

Claudia’s diary

Being the only member of her small coven to be turned into a vampire as a child, Claudia had no human nor vampire companions who could truly understand her struggles. In search of an emotional outlet, she took up writing a diary, where she would explore her deepest thoughts and feelings, secret even to her adoptive fathers, Louis and Lestat. Unfortunately, these diary entries would be used to convict her of braking the vampiric rules of Armand’s coven and soon become one of the only reminders of her existence.

Claudia’s mask from the Mardi Gras party

In her early years, Claudia, searching for companionship, attempted to turn her mortal boyfriend into a vampire. After failing, she begged Lestat to fulfill her wish but was denied and forced to watch the boy perish.

After enduring more years of abuse at the hands of their vampiric creator, Claudia would finally convince Louis that they needed to free themselves from his tyrannical grip. Her plan began with convincing Lestat to hold a party at their home to commemorate their upcoming departure from New Orleans; here, Claudia would use poisoned blood to subdue him, readying him for Louis, who would deal the fatal blow.
