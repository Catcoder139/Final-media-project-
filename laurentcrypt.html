Laurent’s jacket

This is the jacket Laurent wore during his time in Forks, Washington, where he and his nomadic coven ruthlessly hunted and fed on the town’s population. The small coven consisted of only three vampires: Laurent, Victoria and their leader, James.

It was on a hunt, that the coven came across the Cullen vampires (who fed only on animals) and their human friend, Bella Swan. After the encounter, James decided that Bella would become his next prey and soon began his obsessive hunt for her. Despite his loyalty to his coven, Laurent feared the retaliation of the Cullen vampires, making the decision to warn them of James’ plan to kill Bella.

A photo of Irina

After James was killed by the Cullen vampires, Laurent left his coven, leaving only Victoria, James’ lover. After travelling for some time, he was taken in by the Denali coven, where he met Irina and quickly fell in love with her.

The Denali coven, like the Cullen family, hunted only animals, which gave their eyes a light amber colour, unlike human-feeding vampires who possessed bright red eyes that would deepen into black when they were hungry. Despite his love for Irina, Laurent did not subscribe to the Denali coven’s diet and continued to feed on humans in his own time. It was this unwavering lust for human blood that eventually led to Laurent meeting his end.

Volturi flag

A flag bearing the crest of the Volturi, the largest and most powerful coven of vampires. In his desire for power, Laurent wished to join the Volturi, but was rejected after being declared untrustworthy.
